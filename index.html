The most concerning part of a mirage situation, is that you hallucinate the things you need the most. You know these things exist, they are part of your world, but for some reason they are entirely unattainable. The stories enclosed are delusional yet they are real. They’re signs of a utopian world that we’re not allowed to get to. Hints of possibilities, balanced with reality.
